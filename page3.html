How To Help

  • Educate your family and friends about endangered species. Talk with others to make a change.
  • Recycle and Buy Sustainable products to help the environment that animals live in. For example, Don't buy furniture with wood from a rainforest.
  • Walk More, Drive Less. Take public transportation to get to places. Do the most that you can to reduce your personal footprint.

  • Fight to end climate change. Climate change is destroying crucial habitats, especially for polar bears. Sign petitions online, organize a protest, or simply educate others about the topic.
